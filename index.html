    About us

    Ruthshield Limited is a professional consulting firm that provides Security Systems Solutions and Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) services. The company is committed to the preservation of Security Systems Solutions and Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) when performing their operations in Nigeria. Ruthshield Limited has prepared an outstanding professional team to handle its Security Systems Solutions and Health, Safety and Environmental Management System to obtain their assigned and planned Objective. The firm was registered under the Companies Allied Matters Decree of 1990 (RC 1819237) with a commitment to provide efficient and quality service to it's Clients. Ruthshield Limited is located at Ilasan, Lekki, Lagos.

    Mission Statement

    To be the leading Security Systems Solutions and Health, Safety, Environment provider, providing outstanding clients experience through a bespoke service tailored to their specific needs, ultimately delivering superior stakeholder value.

    Vision

    We are committed to the ongoing improvement of the services we provide to our clients, by investing and developing our most important assets “our staffs”, we constantly exceed our client expectations by delivering the best and safe quality services through our professional and motivated team. It is our vision to earn the trust of our clients through value-adding and excellent service performance in all of our operation within Nigeria, Africa and across the world.
